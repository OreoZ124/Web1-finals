BIOGRAPHY

Profile

Ever bright and cheerful, she loves festivals more than anyone else. Her innate altruism makes her unable to ignore a person in need,

and she's always ready to lend an ear. Her bottomless kindness has earned her the admiration of those around her she continues to run towards the dazzling horizon,

hoping that she will discover her true dream along the way.

Appearance

She wears a black, short kimono-styled dress with red and gold accents, that have a little opening that show her cleavage.

The kimono has detached, flounce-style sleeves decorated with Japanese flowers and black cloud patterns. She has a red and white obi at her waist, with a large shimenawa bow at the back. She wears thigh-high black stockings with red accents and gold details, along with red and black platform sandals.
